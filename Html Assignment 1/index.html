HARRY STYLES

Having made its debut the same time Harry Styles first took the Coachella stage, “Late Night Talking” was officially released alongside the artist’s third album, Harry’s House. With a “classic, 70s-esque vibe,” the track is rumored to have been written about Olivia Wilde, Styles’ partner at the time the two were on tour.
